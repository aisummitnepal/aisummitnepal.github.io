About the Summit

AI Summit Nepal 2025 is a hybrid conference + unconference uniting policymakers, industry leaders, researchers, startups, and AI enthusiasts. Join us to co-create Nepal’s AI-powered future through interactive sessions, workshops, and networking.

Two-Day Agenda

Day 1: Exploration, Inspiration & Knowledge Sharing including keynotes, breakout sessions, mentor circles, and cultural exchange.

Day 2: Creation, Demonstration & Future Building featuring youth ideation pitches, mega-panels, awards, and the Summit Declaration.
