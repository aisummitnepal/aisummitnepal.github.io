About the Summit

AI Summit Nepal 2025 is a hybrid conference + unconference designed to unite policymakers, industry leaders, academic researchers, startups, and AI enthusiasts. The event emphasizes co-created knowledge sharing, policy shaping, and youth innovation to build Nepal’s AI-powered future.

Why AI Matters

  • Global Impact: Drives innovation, efficiency, and inclusion across sectors.
  • For Nepal: Bridges gaps in health, agriculture, and finance. Enables digital growth.

Event Highlights

  • Interactive keynotes in Agriculture, Health, and Fintech
  • Youth ideation and prototype competition
  • Breakout sessions with real-time co-creation
  • Networking lounges and expert meetups
  • Poster and paper presentations

Two-Day Agenda

Day 1: Opening ceremony, keynote sessions, breakout discussions, mentor circles, cultural evening.

Day 2: Ideation pitch, expert panel, awards, and summit declaration.
