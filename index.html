AI Summit Nepal 2025

14–15 Ashad 2082 | Illam, Koshi Province, Nepal

About Objectives Structure & Format Participants Agenda Sponsorship Contact

About the Summit

AI Summit Nepal 2025 is a hybrid conference + unconference uniting policymakers, industry leaders, researchers, startups, and AI enthusiasts. Join us to co-create Nepal’s AI-powered future through interactive sessions, workshops, and networking.

Objectives

Innovation & Learning

  • Showcase Innovations: Highlight scalable AI applications in agriculture, healthcare, and fintech.
  • Empower Community-Driven Learning: Enable participants to co-create session agendas.
  • Youth Ideation & Prototyping: Mentor 90 students through design-thinking and prototyping.

Policy & Ecosystem

  • Policy Influence: Facilitate dialogues on AI ethics and regulation for Nepal.
  • Foster Partnerships: Build alliances among academia, startups, and policymakers.
  • Sustain Growth: Launch a national AI community platform and funding pathways.
  • Encourage New Talent: Provide visibility and mentorship for early-stage AI ventures.

Event Structure & Format

This summit combines structured knowledge exchange with open, participant-driven exploration.

  • Hybrid Conference + Unconference: Keynotes and panels paired with participant-led sessions.
  • Breakout Sessions: 30-person groups focusing on Agriculture, Health, and Fintech challenges and prototyping.
  • Poster Competition: Youth and professionals pitch ideas; winners receive seed funding and incubation.
  • Keynotes & Panels: 3 speakers per sector share insights; stakeholder panels co-design ethics and governance frameworks.
  • Networking: Themed lounges, QR-link zones, meet-the-expert sessions, and informal dinners.
  • Final Presentations & Closing: Ideation pitches, keynote reflection, and awards ceremony.

Participant Selection & Logistics

Target Participants

90 student innovators (30 per theme). Selections based on merit, motivation, and diversity criteria.

Selection Process

  • Evaluation: Motivation, thematic interest, and commitment.
  • Shortlist: Announced two weeks prior; includes quizzes, forums, and webinars.

Transportation & Accommodation

  • Transport: Buses from Biratnagar and Birtamode hubs to arrive by 8:00 AM.
  • Accommodation: Secure lodging in Birtamode; nutritious meals and a hospitality desk.
  • Sustainability: Digital resources, reusable merchandise, and waste-sorting kits.

Two-Day Agenda

Day 1: Exploration, Inspiration & Knowledge Sharing including transit welcome, keynotes, breakouts, mentor circles, and cultural dinner.

Day 2: Creation, Demonstration & Future Building featuring energizer breakfast, ideation pitches, mega-panels, awards, and summit declaration.
